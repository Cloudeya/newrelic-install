MindHug is an early-stage mental wellbeing start-up supported and incubated by UCL Innovation and Enterprise. We believe that mental health is an important and often overlooked aspect of overall health. We envision creating a platform for people to have more choice in activities and better ownership over their mental health and wellbeing i.e. think art therapy, meditation, pet therapy, etc. We want to offer greater choice, credibility and engagement as well as provide the tools and data to help individuals find what works best for them.
