Mobile Developer (Summer Intern)

Who We are

MindHug is an early-stage mental wellbeing start-up supported and incubated by UCL Innovation and Enterprise. We believe that mental health is an important and often overlooked aspect of overall health. We envision creating a platform for people to have more choice in activities and better ownership over their mental health and wellbeing i.e. think art therapy, meditation, pet therapy, etc. We want to offer greater choice, credibility and engagement as well as provide the tools and data to help individuals find what works best for them. We are currently a small team and looking to grow the team as we develop our MVP and have a number of events in the pipeline.

Where Are We?

We are part of the Hatchery, UCL’s incubator program and work at their co-working space, BaseKX - set in vibrant and innovation-friendly Kings Cross, moments away from Regents Canal, Granary Square and Google. The Hatchery is a programme designed to support the most promising entrepreneurs and startups.

Your Role

As anyone working in a startup will tell you - everyone wears multiple hats multiple times a day! Flexibility is therefore key that also means there is never a dull moment. You will be working closely with the founders and among other things, will be involved in:

  • Development & Maintenance of Mobile Application(s).
  • Helping with all things tech: Software Development, Web Development, Cloud Architecture etc.
  • Assisting with research and product development.
  • Getting to grips with strategy, business planning, financial modelling and fundraising.
  • Helping with marketing, branding and social media activities.

Technical Skills of Ideal Candidate

  • Mobile App Development (Hybrid/Native).
  • Hands-on experience with Apache Cordova/React Native/Flutter.
  • Python (Flask/Django).
  • Ability to work with REST APIs.

Essential

We are looking for enthusiastic, creative, eager to learn, and driven individuals who are currently enrolled in any year of their undergraduate degree(CSE/ECE). This will be on a full-time basis for 2-3 months starting from May. We want the right person and are flexible about how this person works. Good interpersonal and communication skills, experience in App Development, an interest in innovation and a passion for mental wellbeing are a must.

Nice to have (but not essential!)

  • Any experience with Ember JS.
  • Knowledgeable in UX/UI.
  • Data science knowledge is a plus.
  • Some experience in psychology, psychiatry and/or medical sciences.
  • Some exposure to GDPR, medical regulations, and NHS and NICE guidelines.
  • Previous work in a startup will be looked at favourably.

MindHug is an equal opportunity employer committed to diversity and inclusion. We encourage qualified applications from individuals with a wide array of backgrounds irrespective of gender, age, race, religion or belief system, sexual orientation and disability. Our people make us who we are.

What We Offer

This is an incredible opportunity to join a new team, build a product and develop various skills whilst networking with other entrepreneurs - and learn all about innovation and entrepreneurship. You will gain broad experience in developing technology, business strategy, operations, as well as client-facing and desk-based communication. This is a paid remote-internship (work from home!) with a stipend of Rs. 5000-8000 per month. If this internship ticks the boxes for you, we would love to hear from you.
